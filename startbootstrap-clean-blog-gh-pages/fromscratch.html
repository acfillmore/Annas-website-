FISH

  • Facts about fish:
  • Incredible Speed: The sailfish, swordfish, and black marlin are the fastest fish in the ocean, capable of reaching speeds of up to 70 mph
  • Ancient Existence: Fish have been on Earth for more than 450 million years, predating the dinosaurs and mammals.
  • Surprising Lifespans: The Greenland shark is the longest-living vertebrate, with one estimated to be around 400 years old.
